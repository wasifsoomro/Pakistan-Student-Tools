About Air University

Air University is a federally chartered public university in Islamabad, established by Pakistan Air Force. It has campuses in Islamabad and Multan.

Popular Programs

  • BS Electrical Engineering
  • BS Mechanical Engineering
  • BBA

Example Calculation

Matric: 900/1100 (81.82%) | Inter: 850/1100 (77.27%) | Test: 68/100 (68%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Air University conduct its own test?

Yes, Air University conducts its own entry test. It also accepts NAT scores for some programs.

Is Air University only for PAF families?

No, Air University is open to all Pakistani students. It is a public university.

Is Air University affiliated with PAF?

Yes, Air University is a project of the Pakistan Air Force (PAF) and was established by the PAF to promote higher education, especially in aerospace and technology fields.

What programs does Air University offer?

Air University offers programs in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, BBA, and more.
