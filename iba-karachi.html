About IBA Karachi

IBA Karachi is Pakistan's premier business school, established in 1955. It is the oldest business school outside of North America and offers world-class programs.

Programs Offered

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • BS Computer Science
  • BS Accounting & Finance
  • BS Economics & Mathematics
  • BS Social Sciences

IBA Aptitude Test

IBA conducts its own aptitude test which includes English, Mathematics, Analytical Reasoning, and Current Affairs sections. The test is considered one of the toughest university entry tests in Pakistan.

Important Note

IBA's exact weightage formula is not publicly disclosed. This calculator provides an estimate. IBA also considers extracurriculars and interviews for some programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What score do I need on the IBA test?

There is no fixed minimum — it depends on overall merit each year. Typically 55%+ is competitive.

Does IBA accept SAT scores?

Yes, IBA accepts SAT scores as an alternative to the IBA aptitude test for some programs.

Is IBA public or private?

IBA Karachi is a public university with relatively affordable fee structure compared to private business schools.

How do I prepare for the IBA aptitude test?

Focus on English comprehension, quantitative reasoning, analytical skills, and current affairs. IBA's test is known for being conceptual rather than textbook-based.

Does IBA offer financial aid?

Yes, IBA has a comprehensive financial aid program. Over 60% of students receive some form of financial assistance including need-based and merit-based scholarships.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your IBA Karachi admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your Inter/A-Level obtained/total marks and IBA Test obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
