How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your Bahria University admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your HSSC obtained/total and Bahria University Entry Test obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
  2. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Aggregate" button to compute your weighted aggregate.
  3. Review breakdown: The result shows your overall aggregate percentage along with a detailed breakdown of each component's contribution.

The formula used is: 50% HSSC + 50% Entry Test
