KPK Education Boards

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa has 8 boards of intermediate and secondary education:

  • BISE Peshawar — Covers Peshawar and surrounding districts
  • BISE Mardan — Covers Mardan, Charsadda, Nowshera
  • BISE Swat — Covers Swat, Shangla, Buner, Upper & Lower Dir
  • BISE Bannu — Covers Bannu, Lakki Marwat, North & South Waziristan
  • BISE DI Khan — Covers Dera Ismail Khan, Tank
  • BISE Malakand — Covers Malakand Division
  • BISE Kohat — Covers Kohat, Hangu, Karak, Orakzai
  • BISE Abbottabad — Covers Abbottabad, Haripur, Mansehra

KPK Matric Grading System

All KPK boards follow the same grading criteria set by the KPK government. The total marks for Science group students are 1100 and for Arts/Humanities group are 1050.

  • A+: 80% and above
  • A: 70% – 79%
  • B: 60% – 69%
  • C: 50% – 59%
  • D: 40% – 49%
  • E: 33% – 39% (Pass)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the grading system same across all KPK boards?

Yes, all 8 KPK boards follow the same grading system and examination pattern as mandated by the KPK Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education.

What is the passing percentage in KPK boards?

The passing marks are 33% of total marks for each subject and overall.
