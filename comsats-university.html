About COMSATS University

COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) is a public university established under COMSATS (Commission on Science and Technology for Sustainable Development in the South). Campuses:

  • Islamabad (Main)
  • Lahore
  • Abbottabad
  • Wah
  • Sahiwal
  • Attock
  • Vehari

Popular Programs

  • BS Computer Science
  • BS Software Engineering
  • BS Electrical Engineering
  • BS Bioinformatics
  • BBA / BS Accounting & Finance

Example Calculation

Matric: 900/1100 (81.82%) | Inter: 850/1100 (77.27%) | NTS: 72/100 (72%)

Aggregate = (81.82 × 0.10) + (77.27 × 0.40) + (72 × 0.50) = 8.18 + 30.91 + 36 = 75.09%

Frequently Asked Questions

Which NTS test is required for COMSATS?

COMSATS conducts its own entry test or accepts NTS-NAT scores. Check the latest admission policy for your preferred campus.

What is the minimum percentage required?

Generally 60% in Matric and Inter is the minimum eligibility. Competitive programs may require higher merit.

Is COMSATS a public or private university?

COMSATS is a public sector university, so fee structure is relatively affordable compared to private universities.

How many campuses does COMSATS have?

COMSATS has 7 campuses across Pakistan: Islamabad, Lahore, Abbottabad, Wah, Sahiwal, Attock, and Vehari.

Is COMSATS a public or private university?

COMSATS is a public university established under a presidential ordinance. It is funded by the government and recognized by HEC.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your COMSATS University admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your Matric obtained/total, Inter obtained/total, and NTS/NAT obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
  2. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Aggregate" button to compute your weighted aggregate.
  3. Review breakdown: The result shows your overall aggregate percentage along with a detailed breakdown of each component's contribution.
