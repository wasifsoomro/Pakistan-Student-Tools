About IST

Institute of Space Technology (IST) is a federal university in Islamabad under SUPARCO (Space and Upper Atmosphere Research Commission). It specializes in space science and engineering.

Programs Offered

  • BS Electrical Engineering
  • BS Mechanical Engineering
  • BS Avionics Engineering
  • BS Computer Science
  • BS Space Science
  • BS Materials Science

Frequently Asked Questions

Is IST expensive?

IST has relatively low fees as a federal institution. Some students receive SUPARCO scholarships.

What is IST known for?

IST is known for space science, satellite engineering, and avionics — unique programs not offered elsewhere in Pakistan.

What is IST known for?

IST (Institute of Space Technology) is Pakistan's premier space sciences university, affiliated with SUPARCO. It offers specialized programs in space science, satellite engineering, and related fields.

Where is IST located?

IST's main campus is in Islamabad. It offers programs in Aerospace Engineering, Avionics Engineering, Materials Science, and Remote Sensing.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your IST (Institute of Space Technology) admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your HSSC obtained/total and IST Entry Test obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
  2. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Aggregate" button to compute your weighted aggregate.
  3. Review breakdown: The result shows your overall aggregate percentage along with a detailed breakdown of each component's contribution.

The formula used is: 50% HSSC + 50% IST Test

Why Use This Calculator?

  • Plan your preparation: Know what IST Entry Test score you need to reach your target aggregate before the exam.
  • Compare scenarios: Try different mark combinations to see how improving in one area affects your overall aggregate.
  • Save time: Get instant, accurate results instead of manually calculating weighted percentages.
