About AJK Board (BISE Mirpur)

The Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education Mirpur is the education board for the Azad Jammu & Kashmir region. It conducts Matric (SSC) and Intermediate (HSSC) examinations for students across all AJK districts:

  • Mirpur — Board headquarters
  • Muzaffarabad — AJK capital
  • Kotli
  • Bagh
  • Rawalakot (Poonch)
  • Bhimber
  • Haveli
  • Neelum
  • Sudhnoti
  • Hattian Bala

AJK Board Grading System

AJK Board follows a grading system similar to Punjab boards. The total marks for Matric Science group are 1100.

  • A+: 80% and above
  • A: 70% – 79%
  • B: 60% – 69%
  • C: 50% – 59%
  • D: 40% – 49%
  • E: 33% – 39% (Pass)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AJK Board recognized by HEC?

Yes, AJK Board (BISE Mirpur) is fully recognized by HEC and all Pakistani universities accept AJK Board certificates for admissions.

Can AJK students apply to Pakistani universities?

Yes, AJK students can apply to all Pakistani universities including NUST, FAST, COMSATS, UET, and others on the same merit criteria.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to calculate your AJK Board Matric percentage:

  1. Enter your subjects: The calculator starts with 5 default subject rows. Add or remove subjects as needed using the "+ Add Subject" button.
  2. Fill in marks: For each subject, enter your obtained marks and total marks. The default total is 100 — change it to match your subject's total marks.
  3. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Percentage" button to see your total obtained marks, total maximum marks, and overall percentage.
  4. Review results: Your percentage will appear below along with total marks obtained out of maximum possible marks.
