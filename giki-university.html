About GIKI

GIK Institute (Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute) is a private engineering university in Topi, Swabi, KPK. It consistently ranks among Pakistan's top 5 engineering universities.

Programs Offered

  • BS Electrical Engineering

  • BS Computer Science

Example Calculation

FSc: 960/1100 (87.27%) | GIKI Test: 150/200 (75%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does GIKI conduct its own test?

Yes, GIKI conducts its own entry test covering Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English.

What is the fee structure at GIKI?

GIKI is a private university with higher fees than public universities. Check the official website for current fee structure.

Is GIKI residential?

Yes, GIKI is a fully residential campus. All students live on campus during their studies.

Where is GIKI located?

GIKI is located in Topi, Swabi District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. It has a residential campus spread over 400 acres.

Is GIKI worth the fee?

GIKI is considered one of Pakistan's top engineering universities with strong industry connections and high graduate employability, which many students find justifies the investment.
