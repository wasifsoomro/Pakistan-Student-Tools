About PIEAS

PIEAS is a highly selective federal university in Islamabad, established by the Pakistan Atomic Energy Commission (PAEC). It is known for nuclear engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science programs.

Programs Offered

  • BS Electrical Engineering
  • BS Mechanical Engineering
  • BS Computer & Information Sciences
  • BS Nuclear Engineering
  • BS Physics

Example Calculation

Matric: 980/1100 (89.09%) | FSc: 950/1100 (86.36%) | PIEAS Test: 150/200 (75%)

Aggregate = (89.09 × 0.15) + (86.36 × 0.25) + (75 × 0.60) = 13.36 + 21.59 + 45 = 79.95%

Frequently Asked Questions

How hard is the PIEAS entry test?

PIEAS test is considered very difficult — it covers advanced Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, and English at a level higher than FSc.

Is PIEAS free?

PIEAS has very low tuition fees as it is a federal institution. Many students also receive stipends.

What is the acceptance rate at PIEAS?

PIEAS admits roughly 200-300 students per year, making it one of the most selective universities in Pakistan.

What makes PIEAS unique?

PIEAS is affiliated with the Pakistan Atomic Energy Commission (PAEC) and offers specialized programs in nuclear engineering, mechanical engineering, and computer science with strong research focus.

Is PIEAS admission very competitive?

Yes, PIEAS is extremely competitive. With limited seats and a rigorous entry test, only students with very high scores in both FSc and the PIEAS test are selected.
