FBISE Grading System Explained — Marks, Grades & Percentage

The Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE) serves students in Islamabad and federal areas. If you're an FBISE student, understanding how your marks translate to grades and percentages is essential for university admissions. This guide breaks it all down.

FBISE Grade Scale

Grade Percentage Range Description
A1 80% and above Exceptional
A 70–79% Excellent
B 60–69% Very Good
C 50–59% Good
D 40–49% Satisfactory
E 33–39% Pass
F Below 33% Fail

FBISE Matric (SSC) Total Marks

FBISE Matric total marks depend on your group:

Science Group

  • English: 75 marks
  • Urdu: 75 marks
  • Mathematics: 75 marks
  • Physics: 75 marks
  • Chemistry: 75 marks
  • Biology/Computer Science: 75 marks
  • Islamiat: 50 marks
  • Pakistan Studies: 50 marks
  • Total: 550 marks (theory only)

With practical exams, the total increases. Check your mark sheet for exact totals.

Arts/Humanities Group

The total marks differ based on elective subjects chosen. Typically ranges from 550 to 600 marks.

FBISE Intermediate (HSSC) Total Marks

Pre-Engineering (Part 1 + Part 2)

  • English: 100 marks (50+50)
  • Urdu: 100 marks (50+50)
  • Physics: 100 marks (50+50)
  • Chemistry: 100 marks (50+50)
  • Mathematics: 100 marks (50+50)
  • Islamiat: 50 marks (Part 1 only)
  • Pakistan Studies: 50 marks (Part 2 only)
  • Total: 600 marks (theory, approximate)

How FBISE Percentage Is Calculated

The calculation is straightforward:

Percentage = (Obtained Marks / Total Marks) × 100

For example, if you scored 460 out of 550 in Matric:

460 / 550 × 100 = 83.64% (Grade A1)

FBISE vs Other Boards

FBISE is often considered slightly more challenging than provincial boards:

  • Paper difficulty: FBISE papers tend to be more conceptual compared to Punjab or Sindh boards
  • Marking: FBISE is known for stricter checking — average marks tend to be slightly lower
  • Recognition: FBISE is equally recognized for university admissions across Pakistan
  • Total marks: FBISE may have different totals than provincial boards (e.g., 550 vs 1100 for some boards)

FBISE Results and Grade Improvement

Checking Results

FBISE results are available on the official FBISE website (fbise.edu.pk). You can check results by entering your roll number.

Improvement/Supplementary Exams

  • Improvement exam: If you've passed but want better marks, you can appear in improvement exams for up to 3 subjects
  • Supplementary exam: If you've failed in up to 3 subjects, you can appear in supplementary exams
  • Timeline: Supplementary exams are typically held 2-3 months after regular results
  • Best marks count: For improvement, the higher of your original or improvement marks is considered

Using FBISE Marks for University Admissions

Your FBISE percentage is used directly in university aggregate formulas. For most universities:

  • NUST: Matric 10% + Inter 40% + NET 50% — Calculate NUST aggregate
  • UET: Matric 10% + Inter 40% + ECAT 50%
  • FAST: Matric 10% + Inter 40% + FAST test 50%

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FBISE harder than Punjab Board?

FBISE is generally considered slightly more difficult in terms of paper quality and stricter marking. However, university admissions treat all boards equally — your percentage is what matters, not which board you're from.

What percentage do I need for A1 grade in FBISE?

You need 80% or above to get an A1 grade in FBISE. This applies to both individual subjects and your overall percentage.

Does FBISE give grace marks?

FBISE may award grace marks in certain situations (to reach a passing threshold), but this is not guaranteed and depends on the board's policy for that particular exam cycle.

Can FBISE students apply to Punjab universities?

Yes, FBISE results are accepted by all Pakistani universities regardless of province. You can apply to UET Lahore, NUST, FAST, or any other university with your FBISE marks.
