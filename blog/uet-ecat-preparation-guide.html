UET ECAT Preparation Guide 2026 — Syllabus, Past Papers & Tips

The Engineering College Admission Test (ECAT) is your gateway to public engineering universities in Punjab, including the prestigious UET Lahore. Unlike NET which allows multiple attempts, ECAT is conducted only once a year — so you need to be fully prepared. This guide gives you everything you need.

ECAT Test Structure

  • Total Questions: 100 MCQs
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Mathematics: 30 questions
  • Physics: 30 questions
  • Chemistry: 30 questions
  • English: 10 questions
  • Negative Marking: No — attempt every question!
  • Total Marks: 400 (each correct answer = 4 marks)

ECAT Syllabus — Subject-Wise

Mathematics (30 Questions)

Key topics from FSc Part 1 and Part 2:

  • Algebra: Quadratic equations, partial fractions, binomial theorem, sequences & series
  • Trigonometry: Identities, inverse functions, solutions of triangles
  • Calculus: Limits, differentiation, integration (basic)
  • Matrices: Types, operations, determinants, inverse
  • Coordinate Geometry: Straight lines, circles, conic sections
  • Vectors: Dot product, cross product, applications

Physics (30 Questions)

  • Mechanics: Motion, Newton's laws, work-energy, rotational motion
  • Electricity: Current, circuits, capacitors, Kirchhoff's laws
  • Magnetism: Magnetic fields, electromagnetic induction
  • Waves: Sound, light, interference, diffraction
  • Modern Physics: Atomic models, photoelectric effect, nuclear physics
  • Thermodynamics: Heat, gas laws, kinetic theory

Chemistry (30 Questions)

  • Physical Chemistry: Atomic structure, chemical bonding, states of matter, thermochemistry
  • Organic Chemistry: Hydrocarbons, functional groups, reactions
  • Inorganic Chemistry: Periodic table trends, s-block, p-block elements
  • Analytical Chemistry: Solutions, electrochemistry

English (10 Questions)

  • Grammar (tenses, prepositions, articles)
  • Vocabulary (synonyms, antonyms)
  • Sentence completion
  • Reading comprehension (short passage)

ECAT Aggregate Formula

Your UET merit is calculated as:

Aggregate = (Matric % × 0.10) + (FSc % × 0.40) + (ECAT % × 0.50)

ECAT percentage = (Your ECAT marks / 400) × 100

Month-by-Month Preparation Plan

3 Months Before (April)

  • Finish your FSc Part 2 syllabus revision
  • Start solving topic-wise MCQs for all three subjects
  • Identify your weak topics and mark them for extra practice

2 Months Before (May)

  • Start solving ECAT past papers (aim for 1 paper every 2 days)
  • Focus on your weakest subjects — allocate more time to them
  • Review FSc textbook numericals (many ECAT questions are textbook-based)

1 Month Before (June)

  • Full-length timed practice tests (100 questions in 2 hours)
  • Review mistakes from past papers — maintain an error log
  • Focus on formulas and quick-solving techniques
  • Don't start new topics — consolidate what you know

Last Week

  • Light revision — formulas and key concepts only
  • Get your admit card and check your test center
  • Prepare materials (pencils, sharpener, eraser, valid ID)
  • Sleep well the night before

Top Scoring Tips

  • No negative marking = attempt everything: Even if you're unsure, mark an answer. You have nothing to lose.
  • FSc textbooks are your best resource: A significant portion of ECAT questions come directly from Punjab Textbook Board books. Know them inside out.
  • Time allocation: Spend roughly 35 minutes on each of the 3 main subjects, leaving 15 minutes for English and review.
  • Don't get stuck: If a question is taking too long, mark it and move on. Come back to it later.
  • Read carefully: Many students lose marks due to misreading questions, not lack of knowledge.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is ECAT 2026?

ECAT is typically held in July-August. UET Lahore announces the exact date on their website, usually 2-3 months in advance. Registration typically opens in May-June.

Can I take ECAT more than once?

No, ECAT is conducted only once per year. Unlike NET (which allows 3-4 attempts), you get one shot at ECAT. This is why thorough preparation is essential.

What's a good ECAT score?

For competitive programs at UET Lahore (CS, EE), aim for 300+ out of 400 (75%+). For other programs, 250+ (62.5%) is generally competitive. Remember, your ECAT score combines with Matric and Inter for the final aggregate.

Should I join an academy for ECAT prep?

Academies can be helpful for structured preparation, but self-study with past papers and FSc textbooks is equally effective if you're disciplined. Many toppers prepare on their own using past papers and online resources.
