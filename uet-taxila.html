About UET Taxila

UET Taxila is a constituent college of UET Lahore located in Taxila, Punjab. It has grown into a major engineering campus with its own departments and research centers.

Programs Offered

  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Telecommunications Engineering

Frequently Asked Questions

Is UET Taxila merit lower than UET Lahore?

Generally yes — UET Taxila closing merits are slightly lower than UET Lahore main campus for most programs.

Do I take the same ECAT for UET Taxila?

Yes, the same ECAT conducted by UET Lahore is used for both Lahore and Taxila campuses.

Is UET Taxila the same as UET Lahore?

No, UET Taxila is a separate university with its own campus and administration, though both accept ECAT scores. UET Taxila was established in 1975 as a sub-campus and became independent in 1993.

What programs does UET Taxila offer?

UET Taxila offers programs in Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Computer, Software, Mechatronics, Telecommunication, and Industrial Engineering.
