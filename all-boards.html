About Pakistan's Education Board System

Pakistan's education system is governed by provincial and federal education boards. Each province has multiple Boards of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE) responsible for conducting Matric (SSC) and Intermediate (HSSC) examinations.

All boards follow the national curriculum framework but may have slight variations in subject structure and examination patterns. The grading system and passing criteria (33% minimum) are standardized across the country.
