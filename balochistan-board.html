About BISE Quetta (Balochistan Board)

The Balochistan Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BBISE) is headquartered in Quetta and is the sole education board for the entire Balochistan province. It covers all districts including:

  • Quetta, Pishin, Chaman, Ziarat
  • Kalat, Khuzdar, Lasbela, Awaran
  • Gwadar, Turbat, Panjgur
  • Zhob, Loralai, Musakhel
  • Sibi, Nasirabad, Jaffarabad
  • Nushki, Chagai, Washuk

Frequently Asked Questions

How many boards are in Balochistan?

Balochistan has only one board — BISE Quetta (BBISE), which covers all districts across the province.

What is the passing criteria?

Students need a minimum of 33% marks in each subject and overall to pass the Matric examination.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to calculate your Balochistan Board Matric percentage:

  1. Enter your subjects: The calculator starts with 5 default subject rows. Add or remove subjects as needed using the "+ Add Subject" button.
  2. Fill in marks: For each subject, enter your obtained marks and total marks. The default total is 100 — change it to match your subject's total marks.
  3. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Percentage" button to see your total obtained marks, total maximum marks, and overall percentage.
