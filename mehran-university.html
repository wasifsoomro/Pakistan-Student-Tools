About Mehran University (MUET)

Mehran University of Engineering & Technology is located in Jamshoro, Sindh. Established in 1963, it is the premier engineering university of Sindh province.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MUET only for Sindh students?

MUET primarily serves Sindh students but has open merit seats for students from other provinces.

Does MUET conduct its own test?

Yes, MUET conducts its own entry test for engineering admissions.

Where is Mehran University located?

Mehran University is located in Jamshoro, Sindh. It is one of the oldest engineering universities in Pakistan, established in 1963.

What engineering programs does Mehran offer?

Mehran offers Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical, Computer Systems, Software, Telecommunication, Metallurgy, and several other engineering programs.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your Mehran University of Engineering & Technology admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your HSSC obtained/total and Mehran Entry Test obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
  2. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Aggregate" button to compute your weighted aggregate.
