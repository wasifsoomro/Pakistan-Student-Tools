How to Choose a University

Here are some tips for choosing the right university in Pakistan:

  • Check the entry test requirement — Different universities use different tests (NET, NAT, ECAT, ETEA, SAT). Plan your test preparation accordingly.
  • Compare fee structures — Public universities (NUST, UET, COMSATS) are more affordable than private ones (LUMS, FAST, GIKI).
  • Consider location — Proximity to home, campus facilities, and city opportunities matter.
  • Check HEC ranking — Always verify the university is HEC recognized and check its category ranking.
  • Look at placement records — Check which companies recruit from the university and what salaries graduates earn.
