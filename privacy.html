Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pakistan Student Tools ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user registration, and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number through our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your browser — no input data is sent to or stored on our servers.

We may automatically collect non-personal information through analytics and advertising services, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Operating system

2. How We Use Information

We use the non-personal information collected to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site and which tools are most popular
  • Improve our website's content, design, and user experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Our website and third-party services may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — To track visitor behavior and generate usage reports
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense) — To display personalized advertisements based on your browsing history
  • Functional cookies — To remember your preferences during a session

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website. By continuing to use this website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — Web analytics service provided by Google Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies to analyze how visitors use our site. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — Advertising service provided by Google Inc. AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Protection

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, the risk to your personal information is minimal. All calculator inputs remain in your browser and are never transmitted to us. We take reasonable measures to ensure our website is secure and free from malicious content.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for students of all ages in Pakistan. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since our tools do not require registration or personal data input, no children's data is stored.

7. User Consent

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of our website. Continued use of the website following any changes to this policy will be considered your acceptance of those changes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your browser or Google Ads Settings
  • Clear or disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page. We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
