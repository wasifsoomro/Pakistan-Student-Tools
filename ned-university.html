About NED University

NED University of Engineering & Technology is located in Karachi and is one of Pakistan's oldest engineering institutions (established 1922). It is a top choice for Sindh-based students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does NED include Matric marks?

No, like UET, NED does not include Matric marks in its aggregate formula.

Is NED only for Sindh students?

NED has separate merit lists — open merit and Sindh domicile. Students from all provinces can apply on open merit.

What is special about NED University?

NED University is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering universities in Pakistan, located in Karachi. It is known for its strong engineering programs and affordable fee structure.

Does NED accept students from other provinces?

Yes, NED has provincial and federal quota seats. However, a large majority of seats are allocated to Sindh domicile holders.
