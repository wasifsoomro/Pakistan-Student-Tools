Sindh Board Matric Exam Structure

Sindh boards (BISE Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, Mirpurkhas) conduct Matric exams with total marks of 1100 for science group students. The Sindh board follows a slightly different subject structure compared to Punjab boards.

Key Subjects (Science Group)

  • English (Compulsory)
  • Urdu / Sindhi
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology / Computer Science
  • Islamiat
  • Pakistan Studies

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the percentage calculated?

Your percentage is calculated by dividing total obtained marks by total maximum marks, then multiplying by 100. Formula: (Obtained / Total) x 100.

What are the total marks for Sindh Board Matric?

For Science group, the total marks are 1100. For Arts/General group, the total marks are 1050. This covers BISE Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, and Mirpurkhas.

When are Sindh Board Matric results announced?

Sindh Board (BISE Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, and Mirpurkhas) typically announces Matric results in July-August each year. Check the board website for exact dates.

How many education boards are in Sindh?

Sindh has 5 BISEs: Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, and Mirpurkhas. All follow the Sindh Textbook Board curriculum.

What is the passing criteria for Sindh Board?

Students must score at least 33% in each subject. The minimum passing marks in a 75-mark paper is 25.

Can I rechecking of my Sindh Board papers?

Yes, Sindh boards allow re-checking and recounting of papers. Apply within 15 days of result announcement with the prescribed fee.

What is the grading system for Sindh Board Matric?

Sindh Board uses the same national grading scale: A+ (80%+), A (70-79%), B (60-69%), C (50-59%), D (40-49%), E (33-39% pass).
