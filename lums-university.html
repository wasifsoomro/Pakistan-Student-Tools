About LUMS Admissions

LUMS is Pakistan's premier private university located in Lahore. It is known for world-class business, science, law, and humanities programs.

LUMS Schools

  • Suleman Dawood School of Business (SDSB)
  • Mushtaq Ahmad Gurmani School of Humanities and Social Sciences (MGSHSS)
  • Syed Babar Ali School of Science and Engineering (SBASSE)
  • Shaikh Ahmad Hassan School of Law (SAHSOL)

Admission Tests Accepted

  • SAT (for all schools)
  • LCAT (LUMS Common Admission Test)
  • SBASSE-specific test (for science/engineering)

Important Disclaimer

LUMS does NOT use a fixed formula like other universities. Admissions are holistic — they consider academics, test scores, extracurriculars, essays, and interviews. This calculator provides an estimated academic score only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LUMS have a fixed merit formula?

No, LUMS uses holistic admissions. Academic scores, test scores, extracurriculars, essays, and interviews are all considered.

What SAT score do I need for LUMS?

Competitive applicants typically have SAT scores of 1300+ for SDSB and 1200+ for other schools.

Can I apply with Inter instead of A-Levels?

Yes, LUMS accepts both FSc/Inter and A-Level qualifications. IBCC equivalence is required for A-Level students.

Is financial aid available at LUMS?

Yes, LUMS offers need-based financial aid through the National Outreach Program (NOP) covering 100% tuition and living expenses.

Does LUMS accept both SAT and its own test?

Yes, LUMS accepts SAT scores as well as its own LUMS Common Admission Test (LCAT) for undergraduate admissions.

What SAT score do I need for LUMS?

LUMS does not publish a minimum SAT score, but competitive applicants typically score 1300+ on the SAT. Merit varies each year by program.
