ECAT Aggregate Formula Explained

The University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Lahore conducts the ECAT examination every year as an entrance test for bachelor's degree programs in engineering. The aggregate is calculated by combining your Matric, FSc, and ECAT scores with specific weightages.

UET ECAT Formula (with Matric):

  • Matric (SSC): 25% of your Matric percentage
  • FSc Pre-Engineering (HSSC): 45% of your FSc percentage
  • ECAT Entry Test: 30% of your ECAT percentage

Formula: Aggregate = (Matric Obtained / Matric Total × 25) + (FSc Obtained / FSc Total × 45) + (ECAT Obtained / ECAT Total × 30)

Example Calculation

Suppose a student has the following scores:

  • Matric: 950 / 1100 = 86.36%
  • FSc: 980 / 1100 = 89.09%
  • ECAT: 280 / 400 = 70.00%

Aggregate = (86.36 × 0.25) + (89.09 × 0.45) + (70.00 × 0.30)

= 21.59 + 40.09 + 21.00 = 82.68%

With an 82.68% aggregate, this student is competitive for most engineering programs at UET.

About the ECAT Exam

The Engineering College Admission Test (ECAT) is conducted by UET Lahore for admissions to engineering programs across Punjab. Here are the key details every student should know:

  • Total Questions: 100 MCQs
  • Total Marks: 400 (4 marks per correct answer)
  • Negative Marking: Yes — 1 mark deducted for each wrong answer
  • Subjects: Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, and English
  • Minimum Eligibility: 60% marks in FSc (Pre-Engineering) or equivalent

Since ECAT carries 30% weightage, while FSc carries 45%, maintaining strong academic marks in FSc is equally important as performing well in the ECAT. A balanced preparation strategy across all three components gives you the best chance of securing admission.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ECAT aggregate formula for UET?

UET Lahore uses: Aggregate = (Matric marks / Total × 25) + (FSc marks / Total × 45) + (ECAT marks / 400 × 30). This gives 25% weightage to Matric, 45% to FSc, and 30% to the ECAT entry test.

What is the total marks for ECAT?

The ECAT consists of 100 MCQs with 4 marks each, giving a total of 400 marks. It covers Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, and English. There is negative marking of 1 mark for each wrong answer.

What is a good ECAT aggregate for UET?

For UET Lahore, 75%+ is competitive for most engineering programs. For top programs like CS and Electrical, 80%+ is recommended. Cut-offs vary each year based on competition and available seats.

Does UET use the same formula for all campuses?

UET Lahore, UET Taxila, UET Peshawar, and other campuses generally follow the same ECAT-based aggregate formula. However, merit lists and cut-offs differ by campus and program. Always check the specific campus admission criteria.

What is the minimum eligibility for ECAT?

Candidates need at least 60% marks in FSc (Pre-Engineering) or equivalent qualification (ICS, A-Level with IBCC equivalence) to be eligible for ECAT and UET admissions.
