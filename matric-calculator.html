How to Calculate Matric Percentage

To calculate your Matric percentage, divide your total obtained marks by the total marks and multiply by 100. Our calculator does this automatically for any number of subjects.

Formula: Percentage = (Obtained Marks / Total Marks) × 100

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter each subject name (optional) and your obtained marks.
  2. Adjust the "Total" marks if your subject has a different maximum (e.g. 75 or 50).
  3. Click "+ Add Subject" to add more rows, or the × button to remove one.
  4. Click "Calculate Percentage" to see your result instantly.

Example Calculation

Suppose a student scored the following in Matric Science Group:

  • English: 72 / 100
  • Urdu: 68 / 100
  • Mathematics: 85 / 100
  • Physics: 78 / 100
  • Chemistry: 70 / 100
  • Biology: 82 / 100
  • Islamiat: 45 / 50
  • Pak Studies: 42 / 50

Total Obtained: 542 out of 700

Percentage: (542 / 700) × 100 = 77.43%

Frequently Asked Questions

Which boards does this calculator support?

This calculator works for all Pakistani boards including Punjab (BISE Lahore, Rawalpindi, Multan, Gujranwala, Faisalabad, Sargodha, DG Khan, Sahiwal, Bahawalpur), Sindh, KPK, Balochistan, AJK, and FBISE.

How many subjects can I add?

You can add as many subjects as you need. Click "+ Add Subject" to add more rows.

What if my subjects have different total marks?

Simply change the "Total" field for each subject. The calculator handles varying totals correctly.
