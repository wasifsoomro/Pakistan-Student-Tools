About University of Sindh

The University of Sindh, Jamshoro is the oldest university in Pakistan's Sindh province. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties.

Faculties

  • Faculty of Engineering & Technology
  • Faculty of Science
  • Faculty of Arts
  • Faculty of Commerce & Business Administration
  • Faculty of Education
  • Faculty of Islamic Studies
  • Faculty of Law
  • Faculty of Medicine (Liaquat University)
  • Institute of Information Technology

Campuses

  • Main Campus — Jamshoro
  • Dadu Campus
  • Badin Campus
  • Thatta Campus
  • Laar Campus (Badin)
  • Mirpurkhas Campus

Frequently Asked Questions

Is University of Sindh public?

Yes, it is a public university with affordable fees. It primarily serves Sindh students.

What programs are most popular?

BS Computer Science, BBA, BS English, LLB, and Engineering programs are popular choices.

Does University of Sindh accept students from other provinces?

Yes, there are open merit seats available, but the majority of seats are for Sindh domicile holders.

Where is University of Sindh located?

University of Sindh is located in Jamshoro, Sindh. It is one of the oldest universities in Pakistan, established in 1947.

What faculties does University of Sindh have?

University of Sindh has faculties of Arts, Commerce, Education, Engineering, Islamic Studies, Law, Medicine, Natural Sciences, Pharmacy, and Social Sciences.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your University of Sindh admission aggregate:

  1. Enter your marks: Fill in your HSSC obtained/total and entry test obtained/total marks. Default total marks are pre-filled based on common values — adjust if yours differ.
  2. Click Calculate: Press the "Calculate Aggregate" button to compute your weighted aggregate.
  3. Review breakdown: The result shows your overall aggregate percentage along with a detailed breakdown of each component's contribution.

The formula used is: 60% HSSC + 40% Entry Test

Why Use This Calculator?

  • Plan your preparation: Know what University of Sindh Entry Test score you need to reach your target aggregate before the exam.
  • Compare scenarios: Try different mark combinations to see how improving in one area affects your overall aggregate.
  • Save time: Get instant, accurate results instead of manually calculating weighted percentages.
