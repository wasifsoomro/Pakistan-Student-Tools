Frequently Asked Questions

What is ETEA?

ETEA (Educational Testing and Evaluation Agency) conducts entry tests for KPK universities including UET Peshawar, medical colleges, and other institutions.

Is UET Peshawar the same as UET Lahore?

No, they are separate universities with their own entry tests and merit criteria.

What is ETEA?

ETEA (Educational Testing and Evaluation Agency) conducts the entry test for UET Peshawar and other KPK engineering institutions. It covers Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.

Can ETEA score be used for other KPK universities?

Yes, ETEA scores are accepted by multiple KPK engineering and medical institutions, making it a one-test solution for KPK admissions.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your UET Peshawar admission aggregate:
