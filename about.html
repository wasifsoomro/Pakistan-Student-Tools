About Pakistan Student Tools

Pakistan Student Tools is a free, fast, and easy-to-use online platform built specifically for Pakistani students. We provide simple and accurate tools for calculating marks percentages, university aggregate scores, and CGPA — helping millions of students make informed decisions about their academic future.

Free to Use No Signup Required Works on Mobile Updated 2026

Our Mission

Every year, millions of Pakistani students need to calculate their Matric and Intermediate percentages for college admissions, scholarship applications, and university entry tests. Many students struggle with manual calculations or rely on unreliable sources. We built this platform with one simple goal: to make academic calculations fast, free, and error-free for every student in Pakistan.

Whether you are a Matric student checking your board result percentage, an Inter student preparing for university admissions, or a university student tracking your CGPA — our tools are designed to help you get accurate results in seconds.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive suite of free calculators covering every stage of a Pakistani student's academic journey:

Marks & Percentage Calculators

  • Matric Percentage Calculator — Works for all 28+ Pakistani education boards
  • Inter Percentage Calculator — Part 1 & Part 2 combined HSSC percentage

University Aggregate Calculators

  • NUST Aggregate Calculator — 10% Matric + 40% Inter + 50% NET
  • FAST-NUCES Calculator — NAT-based admissions
  • COMSATS Calculator — NTS-based admissions
  • UET Lahore Calculator — ECAT-based admissions
  • LUMS Calculator — SAT/LCAT-based estimate
  • Plus 10+ more universities — view all university calculators

GPA & Board Calculators

  • CGPA Calculator — 4.0 and 5.0 scale for HEC universities
  • Multi-University Merit Calculator — FAST, COMSATS, UET, LUMS in one tool
  • Board-specific calculators for Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Balochistan, FBISE, AJK, and BISE SBA — view all boards

Why Students Trust Us

  • Accurate formulas — Our calculators use the commonly accepted grading formulas used by Pakistani education boards and universities
  • 100% private — All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store your marks, grades, or any personal data
  • No signup required — Just open and use. No accounts, no fees, no hidden costs
  • Mobile-friendly — Use on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop
  • Regularly updated — We update our tools and content to reflect the latest admission criteria

Important Note

Pakistan Student Tools is an independent educational resource. We are not affiliated with any education board, university, or government body. Our tools provide estimates based on commonly used formulas. Students should always verify results with official sources before making admission decisions.
